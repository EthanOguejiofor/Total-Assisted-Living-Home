About Our Facility

Total Assisted Living Home is a premier senior care facility located in the peaceful community of Fulton, Maryland, offering personalized care in a warm, homelike environment.

Our Mission

Our Care Services

We offer comprehensive care services tailored to meet the unique needs of each resident.

Assisted Living

Personalized assistance with daily activities including bathing, dressing, and meal management to promote independence.

  • Personal care assistance
  • Mobility support
  • Personalized care plans

Memory Care

Specialized care for residents with dementia and Alzheimer's disease, with activities designed to maintain cognitive function.

  • Secure environment
  • Cognitive therapies
  • Trained dementia staff

Health Management

On-site nursing care and medication management for chronic conditions like diabetes, with access to specialized healthcare services.

  • Medication administration
  • Health monitoring
  • Coordination with physicians
